What is Jetstrap?

Jetstrap is a completely web-based, interface-building software solution for Twitter Bootstrap. It is designed for developers and designers to help them create mock-ups and build effective frameworks for design ideas on their website.

Who is it for?

It is designed for developers and designers to help them create mock-ups and build effective frameworks for design ideas on their website. The platform helps to eliminate the time-consuming elements and instead lets you get your design up and running in less time.

Why you'll love it

Enjoy a comfortable code editor with a new widget integrated into the system. One-click on a button “Code” would open a slider with all options to work with code. What is especially effective is a live display of the snippets in the screen panel.
